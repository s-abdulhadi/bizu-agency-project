Neon Light Rebranding

Energizing a startup brand with a fresh, electric visual identity.

Client

Neon Energy Co.

Category

Branding

Date

June 2024

The Challenge

The client was entering a crowded market dominated by big players. Their original branding felt generic and didn't capture the essence of their product. They needed a visual identity that would pop on shelves and Instagram feeds alike.

The Solution

We stripped back the clutter and focused on high-contrast colors against dark backgrounds. We developed a custom look that feels energetic and motion-forward. For packaging, we used matte materials with spot-gloss elements.
